Our Story

More than a restaurant — a taste of home.

How It Began

Welcome to
Grill Master

Grill Master African Restaurant was born from a simple, powerful passion: to share the vibrant, bold, and soulful flavors of African cuisine with our community here in Houston.

Our founder, inspired by the bustling markets and warm family gatherings of his childhood, wanted to create a place that felt like home — where every meal tells a story and every guest is family.

We believe food is a universal language. It's about more than sustenance — it's about tasting, sharing, and connecting.

What Drives Us

Our Philosophy

Every choice we make — from sourcing to seasoning — is guided by these three principles.

The Grill

Our culinary philosophy centers on the open flame. Fire transforms ingredients — it deepens flavor, creates texture, and connects us to ancient cooking traditions.

Fresh & Authentic

We use only the freshest locally-sourced ingredients combined with authentic spices imported directly from Africa. No shortcuts, no compromises.

Community First

A meal at Grill Master is never just a meal. It's a gathering, a celebration, a moment of peace. We cook with heart because our guests deserve nothing less.

Our Craft

Fresh from
the Fire

From our signature grilled tilapia to our savory kati kati, each dish is prepared with the care and precision of a chef who deeply loves their craft.

We source local produce whenever possible and combine it with authentic African spices — many imported directly from Cameroon and across West Africa — to create flavors that are both familiar and surprising.
